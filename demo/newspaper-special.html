Rohan Weds Ananya

Two Families United in Historic Ceremony; Whole Town Invited to Rejoice
By Our Matrimonial Correspondent · Front Page Exclusive

In what observers are calling the event of the year, lifelong sweethearts Rohan and Ananya will be married this December before family, friends and well-wishers from across the district.

📷
Fig 1. The happy couple, photographed at the engagement.

Sources close to the families confirm preparations have been underway for months, with reports of "unprecedented quantities of biryani" already on order.

The ceremony, our correspondent understands, will be followed by dinner, dancing, and celebrations expected to continue well into the night.

Readers are urged to clear their calendars. Full details appear in the classifieds below. This newspaper extends its warmest congratulations.

From the House of Rao
