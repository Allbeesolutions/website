How They Got Here

It started, as the best stories do, by accident — a misdelivered parcel, a knock on the wrong door, and a conversation that ran far longer than either intended. Anjali laughs that she only opened the door to return the package. Vikram insists he knew, even then.

“We weren't looking for forever. Forever simply arrived, unannounced, and refused to leave.”

Five years, two cities, and one very patient golden retriever later, they are exactly where they always suspected they'd end up: about to promise the rest of their lives to one another — and they would be honoured to have you witness it.

The celebration spans an evening of music, feasting and the kind of dancing that does not stop until the lights come up. Come hungry. Come ready.
